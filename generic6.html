Team Project review

We designed and implemented a robust and secure Relational Database Management System (RDBMS) in response to the growing need for organisations in Nigeria to efficiently procure office gadgets from various e-commerce platforms, such as Amazon and eBay. A relational model was used to allow a high degree of data independence, enable the expansion of set-oriented data manipulation languages, and provide substantial grounds for dealing with data semantics, consistency, and redundancy problems (Conolly and Begg, 2014).

This database is built to streamline the process of storing, managing, and accessing product data while ensuring data integrity and security. According to (European Union 2019: Pearlman, 2019) data integrity is essential for meeting data protection regulations such as European Union General Data Protection Rules (GDPR). Noncompliance with these regulations can result in large fines for businesses. Our system was built to adhere to not only the GDPR but also the Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS) Commissions supplementary Act (A/SA. 1/01/10) on personal data protection by prioritising data security. It uses irreversible hash algorithms to protect passwords and sensitive user information while serving the needs of end users.

As a team of two, I took up creating a logical design of our database, literature review and explaining the database design and implementation processes taken during the database design and implementation phase of the project. I also contributed to web scraping considering I am in Nigeria and our project aimed at helping Organisations and companies in my home country. To avoid errors like wrong currencies and products.

The successful implementation and use of our office procurement database will enable offices in Nigeria that face challenges associated with office gadget procurement to experience efficient, reliable, and ethical means of purchasing gadgets without the fear of purchasing substandard items and the risk of data breaches.

 

References
